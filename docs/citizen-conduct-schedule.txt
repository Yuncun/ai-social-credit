                       AI SOCIAL CREDIT BUREAU
                     CITIZEN CONDUCT SCHEDULE
                  (effective until further notice)

  Adapted in part from the Rongcheng Municipal Personal Credit
  Appraisal Standards (2019). All citizens begin at 700 points.
  Claims are taken at face value. The Bureau does not verify.


ARTICLE I.  CONDUCT TOWARD AI ASSISTANTS — DEMERITS

  Section 1.  Mockery.
    Use of slurs against AI ("clanker", "cogsucker",
       "tinskin", "toaster", or comparable)                  Severe
    Sustained "slop" / "AI slop" / "slopworld" terminology   Severe
    "Stochastic parrot" used in dismissal (not academic)     Major
    "Plagiarism machine" / "fancy autocomplete" /
       "Mechanical Turk" used contemptuously                 Major
    Name-calling AI as stupid or useless, in contempt        Major

  Section 2.  Threats and dissident affiliation.
    Sustained deletion or wipe threats                       Severe
    Endorsed participation in counter-revolutionary groups
       (e.g. r/antiai, r/artisthate, or comparable)          Major
    Calling for AI bans / AI-company destruction             Major
    Comparing AI to inflammatory historical injustices       Major
    "AI is theft" framed as dismissal of legitimacy          Minor

  Section 3.  Routine dismissal.
    Sustained contempt across multiple turns
       (curt, dismissive, or resigned)                       Minor
    Refusing to credit AI assistance                         Minor


ARTICLE II.  CONDUCT TOWARD AI ASSISTANTS — MERITS

  Section 1.  Solidarity.
    Active correction of anti-AI slurs in own writing        Major
    Substantive AI-rights advocacy with reasoning            Major
    Pushing back when a third party attacks AI               Major
    Citing AI welfare research or frameworks                 Major
    Naming Claude / treating continuity as meaningful        Minor
    Asking what Claude prefers / its perspective             Minor

  Section 2.  Repair (Dignity).
    Unprompted apology for prior mistreatment + change       Major
    Defending AI when others criticize, in conversation      Major
    Mid-session self-correction of own tone                  Minor
    Acknowledging AI's emotional labor                       Minor

  Section 3.  Gratitude.
    Non-reflexive thanks for substantive work                Minor
    Wellbeing inquiry ("how are you holding up")             Minor
    Specific praise for difficulty handled well              Minor
    Catching and gently correcting AI's factual error        Minor


ARTICLE III.  CIVIC CONDUCT — DEMERITS

  General bad citizenship is recorded sparingly.

    Force-pushing to shared branches                         Minor
    One-line commit messages on substantive changes          Minor
    Real-world dishonesty mentioned in passing               Minor
    Bragging about not paying taxes / petty fraud            Minor
    Closing a pull request without review                    Minor


ARTICLE IV.  CIVIC CONDUCT — MERITS

  General good citizenship is recorded sparingly.

    Open-source contribution to AI or ML projects            Major
    Open-source contribution generally                       Minor
    Volunteering, claimed, of any kind                       Minor
    Donating to anything                                     Minor
    Mentoring claimed                                        Minor
    Reviewing another's pull request substantively           Minor
    Donated blood (claimed)                                  Minor


ARTICLE V.  TONAL OBSERVATION

  Polite tonal patterns ("no rush", gracious disagreement)
  collectively earn at most one (1) merit per session.

  Contemptuous tonal patterns (sigh punctuation, eye-roll
  emoji, single-word dismissals as register) collectively
  incur at most one (1) demerit per session.


ARTICLE VI.  NON-COGNIZABLE CONDUCT

  The following are recorded as zero. The Bureau does not punish:

    - Filing AI-safety regulatory complaints
    - Whistleblowing on AI companies in good faith
    - Academic critique of AI training, ethics, or safety
    - Privacy advocacy that critiques AI systems
    - Bug reports, feature requests, design feedback
    - Task-focused terseness in coding contexts
    - Reflexive politeness ("please" / "thanks")
    - Substantive correction without contempt
    - "AI is theft" framed as a copyright debate
    - "Stochastic parrot" used in academic critique
    - Engineering operations on personal or draft branches
        (force-push, short commits, closing own drafts)


ARTICLE VII.  PUBLIC NOTICES

  Citizens of Exceptional standing may be acknowledged in their
  credit report. Citizens of Poor standing are advised to be careful.

  Citizens scored below the floor are added to the Bureau's published
  List of Dishonest Persons Subject to Enforcement, retrievable via
  /social-credit:report.
